My Story

Welcome to my website! My name is Victor Fettig, and I am an avid collector. I started my Furby collection in 2017, and have been expanding it ever since. Throughout the past 6 years, I've learned a lot about these electronic little friends, and I'd like to share their lore with those who are interested in it. Whether you're a fellow collector, enthusiast, or just someone who's wondering how much your old childhood Furby is worth, you're in the right place!
